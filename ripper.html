The Ripper

The Ripper was my first attempt at creating a short game experience from start to finish. I view the project as a good personal experience in exercising my design and problem-solving skills.

Project began in April 2018 and was finished in one month. It is currently available to play for free in browser.

My contribution to The Ripper:

  • Familiarized self with design, audio, and 2D art pipelines.
  • Designed and scripted a functional dialogue system and puzzle
  • Created all in-game assets
