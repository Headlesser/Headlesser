Passwords, Please

Passwords, Please is currently in development by a team of 3 members. My primary role on the team is as a designer and programmer. This project is part of a campaign to create an educational puzzle game geared towards engaging players with ideas of how people store personal data and how others might take advantage of poor information security habits.

Project began in January 2019 and is currently in ongoing development.

My contribution to Passwords, Please:

  • Communicating regularly with contractors and team members to discuss design space, filter ideas and make concrete decisions
  • Managing tasks and deadlines to uphold a self-motivating work ethic and keep team members on schedule
  • Designing an educational puzzle experience using realistic security habits to create a sense of relatability
  • Developing prototypes for various mechanics and structures such as movement, click interactions, etc
  • Organizing and updating clear and concise documentation
