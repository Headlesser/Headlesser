Enter the Dungeon

Enter the Dungeon is a short text adventure game made during the COVID-19 pandemic

Project began in February 2020 and was finished April 2020. The game can be viewed on itch.iohere

Skills:

  • Familiarized self with design, audio, and 2D art pipelines.
  • Designed and scripted a functional dialogue system and puzzle
  • Created all in-game assets
  • Download on itch.io

One of the problems I had during development was how to display held clues to the player and how to provide a sense of progression, which players said was very unclear during playtesting. I solved these problems by designing a diegetic UI and layered music track to add more instruments with each clue found as a way to audibly and visibly communicate the effects of the player's actions.

This created a situation where the player could audibly hear their progression. That is, with each clue found, the soundtrack becomes more complex until the entire song has been 'revealed' whenever the player has successfully completed a task. The diegetic UI cut down on having to create an extensive amount of menu assets given the limitations of Construct 3 Free Edition, and also made it clearer to the player what items they had on their person at any given time.
