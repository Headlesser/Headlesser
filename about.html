Who is Emilie?

Emilie Holtz - Game Designer

Howdy! My name is Emilie Holtz and I am a Game Designer with an understanding of game prototyping and programming practices, and I am interested in exploring topics in UI/UX, systems design, and all things games.

I love sketching, writing, the color pink, and of course, making impactful and memorable experiences. Whether it is games, stories or the like, I always strive to create things that are more than just what meets the eye.

I have learned many things on my odessey into the realm of design and the games industry: meeting new friends, gaining new perspectives, cultivating new skills and philosophies. Even in my free time I always have something in my hands, be it tablet or pencil. Regardless of the medium I am always creating and ready to learn.

Currently, I am finishing my degree in Game Design and Informatics at Indiana University (class of 2020), and I am always looking for new ways to engage with my passions.
