Who am I?

My name is Emilie Holtz and I am a Game Designer with a thorough understanding of technical practices, which allows me to be a bridge between scripting and designing by developing demonstrative prototypes and crafting engaging elements and mechanics that both adhere to the project's constraints and create an enjoyable player experience.

I am interested in exploring topics in gameplay, puzzle and systems design.

Currently, I am finishing my degree in Game Design and Informatics at Indiana University, class of 2020.

Feel free to contact me on LinkedIn or by e-mail at emiholtz@emilie.info
