Emilie Holtz - Game Designer

OddestSea | August 2018 - Ongoing | Design, Programming

A 3D sailing game where monsters are in constant pursuit and the player's only hope of survival is to reach the lighthouse at the center of a dark and deadly sea.

I work as a designer who provides technical support in the form of scripting and facilitating communications between tech and other members of the team. Click the image for more information.

Passwords, Please |March 2019 - Ongoing | Design, Programming

A 3D puzzle game created for the IT Training Program at Indiana University. The goal of this game is to make players feel aware of their own security practices and how they might change them.

I work as a designer and consultant with contract supervisors to make sure all aspects of the design fall within institution credentials and goals. Documentation on this project can be found by clicking here. Click the image for more information.

The Ripper | April 2018 - May 2018 | Design, Programming, Art, Sound

A 2D dialogue-based puzzle game based on the infamous 19th century serial killer Jack the Ripper and historical figure Inspector Frederick Abberline.

This game was a small passion project to create a fully functional and finished game. Click the image for more information.
