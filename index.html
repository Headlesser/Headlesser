Technical Artist & Game Designer

My name is Emilie Holtz. I am an aspiring woman in technology with experience in Game Design, Prototyping, Technical Art, and Systems Design. As an enthusiast and creative professional, I am always exceedingly eager to meet new people and collaborate with others of all disciplines to create new and inventive experiences. What makes me incandescently passionate about games is the opportunity to work and have fun with a conglomerate of amazing creative professionals, and to forge interwoven and engaging ideas. Every project I have worked on has been a grand experience, and I hope to continue extending my skills in programming and technical art as a woman in games.
