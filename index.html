OddestSea

    A 3D roguelite where the player explores the Oddest Sea, monster-infested world where you must rescue the the sea and its citizens by restoring light the dilapidated lighthouses and driving the beasts away. This game is currently in ongoing development, our ultimate goal is to produce a completed and polished product to sell on the PC market by the end of 2020. Our current design focus right now is how to perfect the sailing experience, such as polishing water shaders, wave choppiness, and open-sea combat.

    My Role:
  • Programming gameplay mechanics such as ammunition, weapons, monster AI, and obstacles
  • Giving artistic critique to team artists so 3D models and other assets accurately portray the wanted player experience
  • Organizing design document notes, meetings, and sprint tasks
  • Designing core themes and mechanics such as moment-to-moment gameplay, upgrade systems, and sailing

Affinity

    An arcade-style local multiplayer fighting game where players compete in fast-paced matches in a retro neon environment. This was a class project for a game production class with a focus on team integration, rapid prototyping, and combat programming. Ultimate project goal was to combine three individual prototypes created by each team member into one unified prototype, in addition to becoming familiarized with AGILE and scrum workflows.

    My Role:
  • Lead programmer, programmed combat, UI and menu mechanics
  • Technical art support with polish elements in the form of particle effects and screen shaking
  • Organized design log document with weekly scrum notes and design concerns
  • Collaborated with teammembers to piece together essential design pillars from 3 different prototypes into one new design
