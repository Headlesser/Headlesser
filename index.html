Passwords, Please

A 3D puzzle game created for the IT Training Program at Indiana University to educate players on password security and identifying common phishing techniques. Join Wolfrid, a sheep-cosplaying wolf and cyber-criminal-extraordinaire, on his heist to crack a safe belonging to an unfortunate individual with poor personal security habits.
