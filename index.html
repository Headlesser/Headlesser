Game Designer & Programmer

My name is Emilie Holtz. I am an aspiring woman in technology with a focus in Game Design, Technical Art, and Programming. As an enthusiast and creative professional, I am always exceedingly eager to meet new people and collaborate with others of all disciplines to create new and inventive experiences. What makes me incandescently passionate about games is the opportunity to work and have fun with a conglomerate of amazing creative professionals, and to forge interwoven and engaging ideas. Game Design Portfolio

OddestSea

A 3D roguelite where the player explores the Oddest Sea, a dark, monster-infested world where you must rescue the the sea and its citizens from darkness by restoring light the derelict lighthouses and driving the beasts away. Ultimate team goal is to have a fully designed, functional, and polished product to release on the PC market. Our main concern right now is how to perfect the sailing experience and fleshing out moment-to-moment gameplay concerns. I work as a programmer and designer currently focused on gameplay mechanics programming.

Affinity

An arcade-style local multiplayer fighting game where players battle in a fast-paced neon environment. This was a class project for a game production class with a focus on team integration, rapid prototyping, and combat programming. Ultimate project goal was to combine three individual prototypes created by each team member into one cohesive, unified game.

Features:
* Retro neon aesthetic
* Rapid and fast-paced matches
* 2 Player Control Support
