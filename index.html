Emilie Holtz - Game Designer

My name is Emilie Holtz and I am a Game Designer with a thorough understanding of scripting. This allows me to communicate and work efficiently with both designers and programmers. I tackle difficult design challenges with a critical eye and a strong attention to detail. I constantly seek out the wisdom of incredibly smart and creative people to strengthen my skills and expand my knowledge on all aspects of design and game development.

A 3D puzzle game created for the IT Training Program at Indiana University. The goal of this game is to make players feel aware of poor personal security habits and how they might identify them.

  • Desiged and developed puzzles and dialogue that are both educational and comedic
  • Worked closely with contractors to provide consulting on industry practices and create a product with requested goals and requirements
  • Managed weekly in-person and remote task distribution and team meetings to keep development going smoothly
  • Organized and updated clear project documentation

Enter the Dungeon | February 2020 - April 2020

Click here for more information

A text-adventure game where you find yourself waking up wet, cold, and alone in a subterranean dungeon and must find a way to escape.

  • Created an unsettling atmosphere that evokes feelings of nervousness rather than blatant surprise or fright
  • Curated visuals and audio to guide the player's actions and provide an additional immersive layer to the text-adventure experience
  • Built upon code to incorporate expanded functionality for images, audio, and input actions
  • Kept a regularly updated design journal/game documentation throughout development
  • Designed 'tree-trunk' method for creating modular puzzles that are both fun for players and friendly for designers
