Game Designer & Programmer

My name is Emilie Holtz. I am an aspiring woman in technology with a focus in Game Design, Technical Art, and Programming. As an enthusiast and creative professional, I am always exceedingly eager to meet new people and collaborate with others of all disciplines to create new and inventive experiences. What makes me incandescently passionate about games is the opportunity to work and have fun with a conglomerate of amazing creative professionals and to forge engaging ideas into awesome interwoven experiences. Every project I have worked on has been a grand experience, and I hope to continue extending my skills in programming and technical art as a woman in games.

Game Design Portfolio

OddestSea

A 3D roguelite where the player explores the Oddest Sea, monster-infested world where you must rescue the the sea and its citizens by restoring light the derelict lighthouses and driving the beasts away. Our current design focus right now is how to perfect the sailing experience, such as polishing water shaders, wave choppiness, and open-sea combat.

My Role:
*Programming gameplay mechanics such as ammunition, weapons, monster AI, and obstacles
*Giving artistic critique to team artists so 3D models and other assets accurately portray the wanted player experience
*Organizing design document notes, meetings, and sprint tasks

Affinity

An arcade-style local multiplayer fighting game where players battle in a fast-paced neon environment. This was a class project for a game production class with a focus on team integration, rapid prototyping, and combat programming. Ultimate project goal was to combine three individual prototypes created by each team member into one cohesive, unified game.

My Role:
*Lead programmer, worked on combat, UI and menu mechanics
*Organized design log document with weekly scrum notes and design concerns
*Collaborated with teammembers to piece together essential design pillars from 3 different prototypes into one new design
