Emilie Holtz - Game Designer

My name is Emilie Holtz and I am a game designer with a thorough understanding of technical practices and the relationship between the programmer and the designer. My background as a technical designer allows me to coherently understand the complications between both designers and programmers, as well as provide advice and create engaging, complex elements and mechanics that adhere to the project's needs.

OddestSea | August 2018 - Ongoing | Design, Programming

A 3D sailing game where monsters are in constant pursuit and the player's only hope of survival is to reach the lighthouse at the center of a dark and deadly sea.

I work as a designer who provides technical support and facilitates clear communication between the design and programming teams.


The Ripper | April 2018 - May 2018 | Design, Programming, Art, Sound

A 2D dialogue-based puzzle game based on the infamous 19th century serial killer Jack the Ripper and historical figure Inspector Frederick Abberline.

This game was a small solo project to both familiarize myself with various development pipelines and software.
